AI Act

    Consult the EU AI Act

    This page helps you navigate the EU Artificial Intelligence Act.

    The Act is divided into 12 chapters, each containing a number of articles, and contains 13 annexes. You can consult the Act by article, open the annexes or use the search function to find specific information.
